üìù Key Notarial Acts

Each notarial act is performed in accordance with Colorado state law to prevent fraud and protect all parties.

Acknowledgments

What it involves: You confirm to the Notary that you signed voluntarily.
What to bring: Document (signed or unsigned) and a valid, unexpired ID.

Oaths and Affirmations (Jurats)

What it involves: You swear or affirm that the statements are true and sign in the presence of the Notary.
What to bring: Document (unsigned) and a valid, unexpired ID.

Signature Witnessing

What it involves: The Notary verifies your identity and watches you sign.
What to bring: Document (unsigned) and a valid, unexpired ID.

I-9 Authorized Representative Services

What it involves: The Notary acts for the employer to examine IDs and complete Section 2 (No notary stamp used).
What to bring: Form I-9, employer instructions, and original current IDs.
